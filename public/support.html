How to Get Help

Common Issues

  • AI agent not joining: Ensure you have entered a valid Google Meet URL and authorized permissions.
  • Canvas not updating: Refresh the add-on or check your network connection.
  • Voice control not responding: Verify browser microphone permissions.

Response Time

We typically respond to support inquiries within 2–3 business days.
